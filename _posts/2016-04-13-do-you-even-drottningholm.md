Do you even Drottningholm?
![](https://the-grid-user-content.s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/ab49026e-bb33-412a-8e18-9f7d3afa6749.jpg)

We've been out to this palace in Stockholm so much. Beautiful place to walk, and let the boys go crazy. The place is packed fountains, gardens and hedge labyrinths. There's a cafe in a 13th century stable where we stop for ice cream if the kids are doing well.
